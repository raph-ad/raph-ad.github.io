Open by private appointment at 8 Gordon Street, Elsternwick VIC 3185

Elsternwick Gardens is situated between Elsternwick and Ripponlea villages, providing convenient access to restaurants, retailers and amenities, alongside various public and private primary and secondary schools. Nearby tram and train lines connect directly to neighbouring suburbs Elwood and St Kilda, and the beaches of Melbourne’s south, while the CBD can be reached in just 15 minutes via train.
